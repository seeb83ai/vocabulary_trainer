Hanzi Movie Method

Configure your personal mnemonic library. Each Chinese character maps to a movie scene: an actor (pinyin initial) in a location (pinyin final), in a specific room (tone), interacting with props (radicals).

Actors (pinyin initials)

Pick a person for each initial. The method suggests choosing people whose names start with the initial sound.

Locations (pinyin finals)

Pick a familiar place for each final sound. Choose buildings you know intimately.

Tone Rooms (tones 1-5)

Each tone maps to a room or area within your location. Defaults follow the Mandarin Blueprint method.

Props (radicals & components)

Each radical maps to a 3D object (prop). Defaults are provided; override or add new ones below.
